Privacy Policy

Your data security and privacy are our top priority

Data Protection & Security

At Neuro Vista, we are committed to protecting your personal and medical data with the highest standards of security and confidentiality. All data transmitted through our platform is encrypted using industry-standard protocols.

  • End-to-end encryption for all patient scans and medical records
  • Secure servers with firewall protection and intrusion detection
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Compliance with HIPAA and international medical data regulations

Your Data Will Not Be Sold

We guarantee that your personal health information will NEVER be sold, shared, or transferred to third parties for commercial purposes.

Your medical data is yours alone. We do not monetize, commercialize, or profit from your health information in any way.

  • No data sales to pharmaceutical companies, insurers, or advertisers
  • No sharing with third-party data brokers
  • No unauthorized access by external organizations
  • Your privacy is protected under strict data governance policies

Data Usage & Purpose

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Processing your MRI scans for Alzheimer detection analysis
  • Generating personalized medical reports and predictions
  • Improving the accuracy of our AI model (with explicit consent)
  • Providing clinical support and chatbot assistance
  • Maintaining your account and scan history

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Right to access all your stored medical records at any time
  • Right to download your scans and reports in standard formats
  • Right to request permanent deletion of your account and data
  • Right to opt-out of any research or improvement initiatives
  • Right to data portability — transfer your records to other providers

Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services and only those that meet strict security and privacy standards. Any external service provider must sign a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) and comply with HIPAA regulations.

Cookies & Tracking

We only use essential cookies for account authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics pixels, or behavioral advertising tools.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personal and medical data is securely erased from our servers within 30 days, except where legally required to retain.
