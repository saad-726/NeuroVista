What does my "Mild Demented" result mean?
N
Mild Dementia (also called Mild Alzheimer's) is the second clinical stage. Key characteristics include:

• Memory Loss: Notable gaps in memory, especially recent events
• Daily Living: Some difficulty with complex tasks (finances, planning)
• Awareness: Patient may still recognize the symptoms
• Recommended action: Neuropsychological evaluation + imaging follow-up in 6 months
